About the game

  • Rock: smashes Scissors and Lizard.
  • Paper: covers Rock and disproves Spock.
  • Scissors: cuts Paper and decapitates (cuts the head off of) Lizard.
  • Lizard: eats Paper and poisons Spock.
  • Spock: smashes Scissors and vaporizes Rock.

"Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock" is an expanded version of a game "Rock Paper Scissors". It was created by Sam Kass and Karen Bryla and popularized by The Big Bang Theory TV Show.
